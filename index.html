    Our Legacy

    Musanze's Pioneering Soul

    Founded in 2003 by the Catholic Diocese of Ruhengeri, Centre Pastoral Notre Dame de Fatima was conceived not merely as a hotel — but as a sacred covenant. A place where every guest arrives as a honoured visitor, and every staff member serves as a steward of community.

    In 2017, the expanded Fatima Hotel elevated the centre to its fullest expression — a 5-star property with modern rooms, two restaurants, multiple bars, five conference halls, and the iconic Volcanic Terrace — while holding fast to the values that define this extraordinary place.

    Common Questions

    Everything You Need to Know

    How close is CPNDF to gorilla trekking? +

    We are 12–13km from Volcanoes National Park — approximately 20 minutes by car. We offer early breakfast from 5:00 AM, boot cleaning service on your return, and can arrange transport. Our concierge team coordinates permits through Rwanda Development Board ($700 pp for foreign tourists — book 3 months ahead).

    What are check-in and check-out times? +

    Check-in is from 14:00 and check-out by 10:00. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — please contact reception in advance. We offer 24-hour reception and a secure luggage storage facility.

    Is breakfast included with my room? +

    Yes — a full breakfast is included with all Classic and Suite room tiers. It features fresh local fruits, Rwandan specialty dishes, continental options and fresh Rwanda arabica coffee. Early breakfast from 5:00 AM is available for trekking guests on request.

    Can I host a conference or wedding here? +

    Absolutely — it is one of our specialties. We have five halls ranging from 30 to 600+ guests, with full AV systems, catering, décor options, and accommodation packages. Our events team is experienced with national summits, corporate retreats, weddings, and NGO gatherings. Use the form below to request a proposal.

    Is there transport from Kigali? +

    Musanze is approximately 96km (1.5–2 hours) from Kigali International Airport. We offer private airport transfer on request — please contact us to arrange and confirm pricing. Regular public express buses also run hourly between Kigali and Musanze.

    Do you accommodate spiritual retreats? +

    Yes — it is central to our founding mission. We host individual and group retreats, pilgrimages to the Fatima Sanctuary, and directed spiritual programmes. Our chaplaincy team is available for spiritual accompaniment. We offer special group rates for parish pilgrimages and diocesan programmes.
